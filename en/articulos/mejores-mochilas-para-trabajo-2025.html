Frequently Asked Questions

What size laptop compartment do I need?

Most work backpacks accommodate laptops up to 15.6 inches. If you have a larger laptop (17 inches), look specifically for backpacks that mention this size in their specifications.

Are expensive backpacks worth the investment?

Higher-priced backpacks typically offer better materials, more features, and longer warranties. For daily use, investing in a quality backpack can save money in the long run.

How important is water resistance?

Water resistance is very important for work backpacks, especially if you commute or travel frequently. Look for backpacks with water-resistant materials or built-in rain covers.

Should I choose a backpack with wheels?

Wheeled backpacks are great for heavy loads or long commutes, but they're typically larger and less versatile. Consider your specific needs before choosing this option.
