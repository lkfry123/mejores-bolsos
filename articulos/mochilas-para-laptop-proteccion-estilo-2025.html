Why Do You Need a Specialized Laptop Backpack?

In today's digital age, your laptop is more than an electronic device: it's your work tool, your entertainment center, and your connection to the world. Protecting this valuable investment requires more than a regular backpack.

Key Points:

  • Physical protection: Padded compartments that absorb impacts
  • Security: Anti-theft features to protect your belongings
  • Functionality: USB ports, organizers and TSA compatibility
  • Durability: Water-resistant and wear-resistant materials

Tips for Choosing Your Laptop Backpack

Consider Your Laptop Size

Make sure the backpack fits your laptop size. Measure your laptop and check the backpack's laptop compartment dimensions.

Think About Your Daily Needs

Consider what you typically carry and choose capacity accordingly. Students may need more space than business professionals.

Prioritize Security Features

If you travel frequently or work in public spaces, prioritize backpacks with anti-theft features and secure closures.

Check for Travel Compatibility

If you travel often, look for TSA-compatible designs and features like trolley sleeves for convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size laptop can fit in these backpacks?

The VOLHER fits up to 15.6", MATEIN up to 17", and NEWHEY up to 18". Always check the specific laptop compartment dimensions before purchasing.

Are these backpacks suitable for air travel?

Yes! All three backpacks are TSA-compatible, meaning they can be opened completely for security inspections at airports.

How do I use the USB charging port?

Most backpacks with USB ports include a built-in cable that connects to a power bank inside the backpack. You can charge your phone through the external USB port.

Are these backpacks waterproof?

The VOLHER and NEWHEY are water-resistant, while the MATEIN offers partial water protection. For heavy rain, consider using a rain cover.
